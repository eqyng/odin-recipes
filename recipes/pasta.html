Dreamy pasta dough

This homemade pasta recipe is our new favorite cooking project! We keep coming back to homemade pasta. It's super fun to make together, and it only requires a handful of basic ingredients. Of course, the fact that it's absolutely delicious doesn't hurt either. ðŸ™‚

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Place the flour on a clean work surface and make a nest
  2. Knead the dough for 8 to 10 minutes
  3. Run the dough through the pasta roller three times
  4. Repeat with remaining dough
